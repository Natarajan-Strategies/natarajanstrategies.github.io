About

Natarajan Strategies is an AI and digital transformation company headquartered in Budapest and serving clients globally. We operate at the intersection of technology, data, product, and business strategy, helping organizations reduce complexity, accelerate execution, and deliver measurable impact. In addition, we provide Educational Consulting service and a comprehensive AI-powered learning platforms that is focused solely on students.

Engagement Model

Clear, outcome-aligned engagement structures designed to move fast, reduce uncertainty, and deliver measurable results.

01. Discovery & Alignment

A rapid assessment focused on understanding business goals, identifying constraints, and defining the strategic opportunity, typically within 2–4 weeks.

02. Strategy & Roadmap

We co-create a pragmatic transformation blueprint covering architecture, data, AI opportunities, operating model, and investment priorities.

03. Pilot, Accelerate & Scale

Targeted initiatives deliver early value fast, building momentum, proving feasibility, and establishing reusable patterns for scale.

04. Leadership Advisory

Ongoing partnership with executives and teams to guide decision-making, mitigate risks, and ensure delivery aligns to strategy and value.
